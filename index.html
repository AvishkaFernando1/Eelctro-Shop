Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available and takes 1-2 business days. Free shipping is available on orders over $50.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be in original condition with all packaging. Return shipping is free for defective items.

Do you offer warranties on products?

Yes, all products come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 1-5 years depending on the product. Extended warranties are available for purchase.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your order status in real-time through our website or the carrier's website.

Do you offer installation services?

We partner with certified electricians in most areas. Contact our support team to arrange professional installation for your purchased products.
